## Internet = The Big Daddy Network

The **Internet is just a huge WAN** ‚Äî millions of networks all connected, talking with common protocols like:  
- **TCP/IP**  
- **DNS**  
- **BGP**  
- **HTTP/HTTPS**

Your phone/laptop = **Client**  
Servers = **Store & deliver content**
